Welcome to the apocalyptic history timeline.

This timeline documents is the work of @jaspergregory, its aim is to gather geodata documenting all known millenarian social movements. The timeline has begun with the Judaic and neo-judaic groups. But the project will eventually be global in scope.

In temporal scope, the project stretches coverss recorded history up to the present, but pre-modern data is very scarce while recent centuries have become progressively more documented.
